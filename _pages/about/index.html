I a 2nd year PhD student in Institut de Mathématiques de Toulouse, Université Toulouse III - Paul Sabatier (now Université de Toulouse), under the supervision of Henri Guenancia and Yuxin Ge.

My research interests focus on Complex Differential Geometry, and Pluripotential Theory. More specificcally, I am currently interested in Kähler-Einstein metrics on singular varieties with log canonical singularities.
