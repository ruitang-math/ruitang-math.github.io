I am a 2nd year PhD student at the [Institut de Mathématiques de Toulouse](https://www.math.univ-toulouse.fr/fr/), Université Toulouse III - Paul Sabatier (now [Université de Toulouse](https://www.univ-tlse3.fr/)), under the supervision of [Henri Guenancia](https://hguenancia.perso.math.cnrs.fr/) and [Yuxin Ge](https://www.math.univ-toulouse.fr/~yge/).

My research interests focus on complex differential geometry and pluripotential theory. 
More specifically, I am currently interested in Kähler--Einstein metrics on singular varieties with log canonical singularities.
