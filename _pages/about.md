I a 2nd year PhD student in [Institut de Mathématiques de Toulouse](https://www.math.univ-toulouse.fr/fr/), Université Toulouse III - Paul Sabatier (now Université de Toulouse), under the supervision of [Henri Guenancia](https://hguenancia.perso.math.cnrs.fr/) and [Yuxin Ge](https://www.math.univ-toulouse.fr/~yge/).

My research interests focus on Complex Differential Geometry, and Pluripotential Theory. 
More specificcally, I am currently interested in Kähler-Einstein metrics on singular varieties with log canonical singularities.
